## Daily questions for Grape

**Analytics & discovery:**

1. "What are the top 3 performing apps this week?"
2. "Which apps have the highest bounce rate and why?"
3. "What countries are driving the most traffic?"
4. "Which apps are getting discovered but not retained?"
5. "What's the conversion rate from discovery to first message?"

**Growth insights:**

1. "Which apps should I promote more based on engagement?"
2. "What traffic sources are working best?"
3. "Are there any apps with high potential but low visibility?"

---

## Daily questions for Pear

**Feedback quality:**

1. "What are the most common complaints across all apps?"
2. "Which apps are getting the most positive feedback?"
3. "What features are users requesting most?"
4. "Are there any critical bugs reported today?"
5. "Which feedback earned the most credits (highest quality)?"

**Improvement priorities:**

1. "What should I fix first based on feedback impact?"
2. "Which apps need the most attention?"
3. "Are there patterns in user frustration?"

---

## Per-app questions (as creator)

### For each app's DNA thread:

**Performance:**

1. "How is this app performing compared to last week?"
2. "What's the average session duration?"
3. "What's the retention rate?"

**User behavior:**

1. "What are users asking most frequently?"
2. "Where do users drop off in conversations?"
3. "What features are being used vs ignored?"

**Quality:**

1. "What feedback has this app received?"
2. "What's the average feedback score?"
3. "What should I improve first?"

**DNA thread health:**

1. "Is the DNA Thread knowledge being used effectively?"
2. "What knowledge gaps exist?"
3. "Should I upload more context files?"

---

## Weekly deep dive questions

**Ecosystem health:**

1. "Which apps are growing fastest?"
2. "Which apps are declining and why?"
3. "What's the overall ecosystem retention?"

**Monetization:**

1. "Which apps drive the most credit spend?"
2. "What's the Plus conversion rate per app?"
3. "Which apps should I monetize more?"

**Builder program:**

1. "Are external builders getting traction?"
2. "What's the revenue share distribution?"
3. "Which builders should I support more?"

---

## Monthly strategy questions

**For Grape:**

1. "What apps should I sunset?"
2. "What new apps should I create?"
3. "What stores need more apps?"

**For Pear:**

1. "What are the biggest pain points?"
2. "What features would significantly improve the platform?"
3. "What's blocking user growth?"

**For the ecosystem:**

1. "What's working that I should double down on?"
2. "What's not working that I should kill?"
3. "What's the next big opportunity?"

---

## Pro tips

**Daily routine:**

- Morning: Ask Grape for overnight analytics
- Midday: Check Pear for new feedback
- Evening: Review individual app performance

**Weekly review:**

- Sunday: Deep dive with Grape on ecosystem health
- Monday: Prioritize fixes based on Pear feedback
- Friday: Plan next week's improvements

**Monthly planning:**

- Review all apps with their DNA threads
- Update app knowledge based on feedback
- Plan new features and apps

---

**Remember:** Use Grape and Pear daily. Validate their insights with data, then ship.
